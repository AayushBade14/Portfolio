Greetings, Explorer!

"What's in it for me?", you say?
Read closely, after reading through this series you will have a complete understanding of how an Animation-System works and you'd be able to build one for yourself. We will be exploring the basics, the math, model-loading, animation-loading(like, duh! otherwise there isn't any point for this series), animation-blendeing and physically-based animations.
